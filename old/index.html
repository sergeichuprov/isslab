Bio

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer and Information Sciences at the Department of Computer Science at RIT.
Before joining RIT, in 2021 I worked as a researcher at the Mobile Intelligent Systems Laboratory at ETU "LETI" University, St. Petersburg, Russia. In 2019 I started my Ph.D. research in Information Security at the Department of Secure Information Technologies, ITMO University, St. Petersburg, Russia. At ITMO, I was a researcher and worked on various interdisciplinary projects (please, see details in my CV). I also teached BS and MS practical courses related to the security and safety assurance in cyber-physical systems, information sciences, and cybersecurity. I participated in the coursework development for the "Functional Securtiy and Safety Assurance in Unmanned Vehicles" Master's degree program. Before that, in 2019 I received a Master's degree in Information Security (with honors), and in 2017 a Bachelor's degree in Information Security at ITMO.
At present, I am working on a security of AI- and ML-based systems, data quality and security assurance, robustness of ML-end systems with integrated network facilities.
